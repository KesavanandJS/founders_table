Common Questions

Who can join Founders Table?

We welcome entrepreneurs, business owners, and founders at all stages of their journey. Whether you're just starting out or scaling to new heights, Founders Table is designed for builders who are serious about growth and community.

How often do events happen?

We organize at least 2-3 events every month, including networking mixers, workshops, mentorship sessions, and special founder forums. Members get early access to event registrations and exclusive discounts.

What is the membership fee?

Event registration fees range from ₹499 to ₹899 depending on the event type and duration. We also offer annual membership packages with exclusive benefits and discounted event access. Contact us for membership details.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Yes, we offer full refunds for cancellations made at least 7 days before the event. Cancellations within 7 days of the event are non-refundable but may be transferred to another event or another person.

How do I stay updated with Founders Table?

Follow us on Instagram and LinkedIn for updates, tips, and exclusive content. Join our WhatsApp community for real-time event announcements and networking opportunities with other founders.
